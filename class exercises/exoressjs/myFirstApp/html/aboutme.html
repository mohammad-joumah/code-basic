About Me

I am an engineer for computer engineering and came to Germany from Syria in 2015 because of the war in my home country.
I completed my computer science studies in Syria in 2014 with a bachelor's degree. I would like to prove to you the knowledge of the programming languages acquired during my studies, also as part of a previous internship.
I took part in a certificate course at BTU-Cottbus-Senftenberg as part of the qualification measure for academics with foreign engineering titles. In addition to specialist seminars, I also attended an intensive language course on work-related German B2, which I completed with level B2.I am now studying in the DCI as part of the qualification measure to learn and experience web development.
